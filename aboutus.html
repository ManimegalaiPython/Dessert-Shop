"The dessert house" would be a dedicated establishment for sweet foods and indulgent range of offerings that include brownies, cakes, desserts, chocolates, breads, and savouries.


From our humble beginnings in 2024, when the first The dessert house pastry store opened its doors at the place of Karaikudi, Tamil Nadu, India, we've grown to become a Pan-India chain of patisseries with stores in over 10 cities.


Our mission is to spread happiness by serving smiles on a plate, and this journey continues as we open The dessert house patisseries across the country.

We as a brand believe in the power of wishes.

Remember the days when you believed anything was possible?

When dreams felt like they were within reach? We want to bring that sense of wonder back to your life.

With every cake we create, we aim to spark joy and remind you that dreams do come true.

At the heart of our journey is constant innovation. Our in-house R & D centre helps us discover new flavours, reimagine classic desserts, and craft experiences that delight with every bite.

This commitment to creativity and quality has not only won hearts but also earned us industry recognition, including winning the Swiggy Award for Best Dessert Brand a milestone that sweetly affirms our mission.
